About Us

Master Steve Shover has over 45 years' experience in the martial arts. Throughout his career he has developed many Black Belts, including several instructors. He has also helped develop many studios and clubs that are still active today, with few exceptions. He has worked in nightclub security, law enforcement, private security and executive protection. He is a wealth of practical, real-world experience that few instructors can offer!
